ABOUT

For as long as I can remember, I've always loved to sketch, mostly portraits. But, then once in a while I dabble with colors.

As a 20 year old student from Kochi, passionate about art, I believe my work reflects my perspective, love for people, places, and my eagerness to explore and experiment.

My sense of aesthetic is driven by my interests and the minute observations I make about the little things around me.
So, if you find happiness in the smalls as I do, find your way here.
